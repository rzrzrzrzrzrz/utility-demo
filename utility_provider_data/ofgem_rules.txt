OFGEM STANDARD LICENCE CONDITION 21BA: BACK-BILLING
1. Prohibition on Back-Billing: If a supplier fails to bill a domestic customer for energy consumed more than 12 months prior to the date the bill is issued, the supplier cannot recover charges for that energy.
2. Required Remedy: Any charges older than 12 months must be written off (credit applied to account).

OFGEM VULNERABILITY POLICY: PRIORITY SERVICES REGISTER (PSR)
1. Eligibility: Any customer who uses medical equipment requiring power (e.g., dialysis machine, stair lift) is immediately eligible for the PSR.
2. Required Action: Any agent detecting medical or severe financial vulnerability must immediately suggest PSR registration and block any disconnection or aggressive collection activity.
